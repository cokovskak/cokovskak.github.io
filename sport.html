From a young age I have been incredibly interested in sports, inspiring me to become a part of the athlete’s world. My first encounter with sports was with playing volleyball in elementary school and since then I couldn’t imagine my life without it. After volleyball I found a new interest and that was Brazilian jiu-jitsu. That sport left a significant impact on my mindset and how I approach stressful situations in everyday life. Although I love these sports, I discovered that my biggest passions are hiking and rock climbing. These sports gave me a much bigger perspective of life. In addition to these sports, I also like cycling, kayaking, running and calisthenics.

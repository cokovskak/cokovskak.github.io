Formal education

After I finished elementary school, I enrolled in the gymnasium SUGS "Orce Nikolov". During those four years of my education, I gained knowledge on different subjects and I decided that I want to study computer science. In 2021, I started studying at the The Saints Cyril and Methodius University in Skopje. My major is internet, networks and security.

Additional skills

In addition to my formal education, I attended many courses and workshops. I successfully passed the FCE exam and finished german classes. I participated in various workshops on topics related to climate change and the protection of the environment. Also, through different online courses I learnt about web design, programming languages and cybersecurity. Furthermore, I have been part of different projects, which improved my soft skills.
